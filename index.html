I, Q by Roland Smith

A bit of a reference, a bit of a fan page

By Sarah Barton

To start off with, here is a brief introduction. This series of six books is centered around a boy named Quest(Q) and his stepsister, Angela. Their parents marry, and the other pair of parrents that are distant from their lives are part of a situation with a terrorist organization. Together, they get sucked into this mystery, attempting to figure out who is in charge and put it down for good. While I can't reveal more unless it is in places with warnings, I hope you decided to read these six books and enjoy them as much as I did.
