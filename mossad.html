Some Rouge Mossad Agents

There is a group of Mossad agents, four specifically, running around in these books. Two dispear pretty quickly, one is actually working for Malak, and the other is the leader and ends up working with SOS. They are one of the villains in book one, before transitioning over to reluctant allies in the following books.
